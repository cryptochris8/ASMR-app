# ASMR Sleep App — Launch Sound Pack Strategy (Claude Code / Product Brief)

## Goal
Define the best sound packs to launch the ASMR sleep app with so the product feels complete, premium, and subscription-worthy from day one.

The initial launch should include:
- a strong free tier
- a compelling premium tier
- clearly themed sound packs
- sounds that are useful for sleep, relaxation, and repeat nightly use

---

# Launch Strategy

## Free Tier
Free users should get enough value to enjoy the app and understand its quality.

Recommended free launch content:
- 2 scenes
- 1 to 2 ambient packs
- limited mixer combinations

## Premium Tier
Premium should feel meaningfully larger and better.

Recommended premium launch content:
- all launch scenes
- all premium sound packs
- advanced mixing
- unlimited presets

---

# Best Launch Sound Packs

## 1. Rain and Window Sounds
### Purpose
This is a sleep category staple and should be part of launch.

### Include sounds like
- rain on glass
- distant soft thunder
- gentle roof rain
- rainy night ambience
- light window tapping

### Why it matters
This is likely your highest-usage pack for sleep.

### Free or Premium
Core version can be Free.
Expanded versions can be Premium.

---

## 2. Cozy Fireplace Pack
### Purpose
Warm, comforting indoor sleep atmosphere.

### Include sounds like
- fireplace crackle
- soft room ambience
- wooden creaks
- blanket or fabric brushing
- low warm air hum

### Why it matters
Adds an emotional “cozy” identity beyond generic noise apps.

### Free or Premium
Premium recommended.

---

## 3. Soft Tapping and Texture Pack
### Purpose
This gives the app an ASMR identity, not just ambience.

### Include sounds like

### Why it matters
Supports the “playable ASMR” concept.

### Free or Premium

Premium recommended.

# Recommended Launch Mix

## Free Launch Content
- Rain and Window basic
- One basic fan / noise option
- Limited tapping interactions
- 2 free scenes

## Premium Launch Content
- Cozy Fireplace
- Expanded Tapping and Texture
- Sand and Tactile
- Night Ambience
- Water and Drip
- Expanded Fan / Sleep Noise
- Cozy Interior Objects

This creates a clear premium value gap.

---

# Content Packaging Strategy

Each sound pack should include:
- base loop
- 2 to 5 interactive accents
- mixer compatibility tags
- volume tuning profile
- category label

Example structure:
- rain_window_base
- rain_window_tap_01
- rain_window_tap_02
- rain_window_thunder_soft
- rain_window_hum

---

# Mixer Compatibility

Mark which packs combine well.

## Good Pairings
- rain + fan
- fireplace + fabric brushing
- night ambience + soft tone pad
- water drip + sand texture
- window rain + tapping

Avoid overly busy combinations by default.

---

# Launch Content Quantity Recommendation

## Minimum Viable Launch
- 3 scenes
- 6 to 8 sound packs
- 20 to 35 high-quality audio assets total

## Better Launch
- 3 to 5 scenes
- 8 to 10 sound packs
- 35 to 60 assets total

Quality matters more than quantity.

---

# Asset Quality Rules

## Must Have
- seamless loops
- stereo where appropriate
- no harsh transients unless intentionally tactile
- normalized volume
- low noise floor
- consistent emotional tone

## Avoid
- harsh clicks
- bright piercing highs
- loop pops
- overly dramatic storms
- distracting melodic content in sleep packs

- habit-forming
